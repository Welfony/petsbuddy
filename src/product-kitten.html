Formulated Kitten Replacement 210g

Description

This professional formula, with bovine colostrum that enriched with immunoglobulins, is able to protect immune and gut system. Meanwhile, this unique replacement can help kittens prevent from nutrional deficiency via high level of taurine content, and also supply essential nutrition with vitamins, minerals and essential fatty acids added.

Specifications

Weight
210g
Ingredients
Whole goat milk solids, vegetable oils, casein, whey protein isolate, lecithin, L-arginine, omega-3 & 6 fatty acids, taurine.
Vitamins
vitamins A, B1, B2, B3, B5, B6, B12, C, D3, E, K, folic acid, biotin.
Minerals
calcium, phosphorus, potassium, sodium, magnesium, zinc, iron, manganese, copper, iodine, selenium
Storage
Mixed Formulated Kitten Replacement can be stored in the fridge for a day or frozen for up to 5 days. Once opened, the rest powder must be refrigerated for up to 3 months, or can be frozen for up to 6 months to preserve freshness.
Directions for use
Success in rearing kittens greatly improves if they receive mother’s milk at birth. Commence feeding milk replacer formula after the initial feeds of mother’s milk. Make up Petsbuddy® FORMULATED KITTEN REPLACEMENT at a concentration of 5g per 15ml of warm water.
