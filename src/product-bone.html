Roo Dental Bone 1pc

Description

It is beneficial for pets to keep dental health. Our 100% natural kangaroo meats are all from local regional farmers and hunters in South Australia, New South Wales and Victoria, and retain all of the natural goodness of authentically fresh kangaroo meat, such as CLAs and PUFA, enriching with unsaturated fatty acids, vitamins and minerals. Every kangaroo treats are high in protein and low in fat to keep your pets healthy.

We do not use preservatives, colour or artificial flavours. All the treats are prepared without cooking and gently air-dried in our kitchen, to lock in natural, delicious goodness for a pure and tasty treat. Great for dogs, and for those who have food allergies or need single protein.

Specifications

Ingredients
Kangaroo Clod Bone
Typical Analysis
Protein > 50% Fat < 8% Moisture < 5%
Storage
Keep treats stay fresh when they are stored in a cool and dry environment. Keep bag sealed after opening.
Minerals
Calcium, sodium, magnesium, zinc, iron, manganese, iodine, selenium, potassium.
Feeding Guide
It is intended for supplemental feeding only, feed as a snack. This means that your pets would love our treats, they still need complete and balanced diet. Always ensure your pet has access to fresh drinking water and shade.
