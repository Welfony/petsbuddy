Formulated Puppy Replacement 210g

Description

This professional formula, with bovine colostrum that enriched with immunoglobulins, can activate immune system of puppies. Also with multivitamins, minerals and essential fatty acids specially added up, it is able to provide nutriton for fast growth. This replacement could protect puppies’sensitive gut through strictly adjusting the level of lactose to resemble dog’s milk.

Mixed formula milk can be stored in the fridge for a day or frozen for up to one week. Once opened, the rest powder must be refrigerated for up to 3 months, or can be frozen for up to 6 months to preserve freshness.
