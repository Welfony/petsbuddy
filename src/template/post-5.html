PetsBuddy™ Pet Products Now Available in China in April 2018

22 Dec 2018 0 comments

Working with our best business partner—Beijing Margo Commercial & Trade Co., Australia Pet Industry Group launched PetsBuddy™ formulated pet dairy products and roo pet treats onto Chinese market through the 5th China Beijing International Pet Supplies Exhibition that has just passed in April. This means that Chinese customers will have access to the highest quality nutrition supplements for hand raising their dogs and cats.

PetsBuddy™ formulated goat milk products for dog and cat are based on the scientific and tested milk formulas that have been successfully used in Australia for decades. Also we carefully choose the best goat milk from Australian and New Zealand farms. They are a nutritionally balanced milk substitute for your dogs and cats of all breeds and highly recommended by many breeders, veterinarians and pet owners.

PetsBuddy™100% natural kangaroo treats are all from local regional farmers and hunters in South Australia, New South Wales and Victoria. All the treats are prepared without cooking and gently air-dried to lock in natural, delicious goodness for a pure and tasty treat. And also all products are preservatives, artificial flavours and colour free.

Beijing Margo Commercial & Trade Co., Ltd, as our exclusive distributor and the most important co-operator in Chinese market, is fully backed by the technical support and manufacturing expertise of Australia Pet Industry Group. In the future, we are planning to provide Chinese customers with more premium pet products.
