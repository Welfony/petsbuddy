Taurine Enriched Formula for Cat 300g

Description

A kind of nutritionally balanced dairy supplement for cats of all breeds. As our unique Taurine Enriched Formula closely resembles cat milk, which formulated to match the average composition of cat’s milk, with added vitamins, minerals and essential fatty acids (Omega-3 and 6). It is perfect to feed orphaned kittens or when the mother’s milk is limited. We also add high levels of the sulphonic acid taurine with only moderate amounts of fat and carbonhydrate to aid in prevention of blindness, hair loss, tooth decay and heart failure.

Specifications

Weight
300g
Ingredients
Whole goat milk solids, vegetable oils, casein, whey protein isolate, lecithin, L-arginine, omega-3 & 6 fatty acids, taurine.
Vitamins
Vitamins A, B1, B2, B3, B5, B6, B12, C, D3, E, K1, folic acid, biotin.
Minerals
Calcium, sodium, magnesium, zinc, iron, manganese, iodine, selenium, potassium.
Storage
Mixed formula milk can be stored in the fridge for a day or frozen for up to one week. Once opened, the rest powder must be refrigerated for up to 3 months, or can be frozen for up to 6 months to preserve freshness.
Feeding Guide
Cooled boiled water Level scoops of powder Number of feeds per day
Adult cats 60-90ml 2-3 1-2
Over 5 weeks kittens 60ml 2 2-3
Pregnant or lactating cats 60-90ml 2-3 4-5

Note:
1 scoop ≈ 5g of powder
1 scoop of powder added to 30ml of water yields approximately 33ml of formula milk.
