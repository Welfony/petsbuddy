Roo Tendon

Description

Make pets more relax and happier.

Our 100% natural kangaroo treats are all from local regional farmers and hunters in South Australia, New South Wales and Victoria. We do not use preservatives, colour or artificial flavours. All the treats are prepared without cooking and gently air-dried in our kitchen, to lock in natural, delicious goodness for a pure and tasty treat. Great for dogs that have food allergies or need new proteins.

Our meats are delivered daily fresh or raw, to retain all of the natural goodness of authentically fresh kangaroo meat, such as CLAs and PUFA, enriching with unsaturated fatty acids, vitamins and minerals. Every kangaroo treats are high in protein and low in fat to keep your pets healthy.
