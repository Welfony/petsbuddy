Roo Tendon 50g

Make dogs more relax and happier. Our 100% natural kangaroo meats are all from local regional farmers and hunters in South Australia, New South Wales and Victoria, and retain all of the natural goodness of authentically fresh kangaroo meat, such as CLAs and PUFA, enriching with unsaturated fatty acids, vitamins and minerals. Every kangaroo treats are high in protein and low in fat to keep your pets healthy.

We do not use preservatives, colour or artificial flavours. All the treats are prepared without cooking and gently air-dried in our kitchen, to lock in natural, delicious goodness for a pure and tasty treat. Great for dogs, and for those who have food allergies or need single protein.
