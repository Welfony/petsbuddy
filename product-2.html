  • Ingredients

    Whole goat milk solids, casein, bovine colostrum powder, whey protein isolate, omega-3 & 6 fatty acids, taurine, choline, inositol.

    Vitamins

    Vitamins A, B1, B2, B3, B5, B6, B12, C, D3, E, K1, folic acid, biotin.

    Minerals

    calcium, phosphorus, potassium, sodium, magnesium, zinc, iron, manganese, copper, iodine, selenium.

  • Storage

    Success in rearing puppies greatly improves if they receive mother’s milk at birth. Commence feeding milk replacement formula after the initial feeds of mother’s milk. Make up Petsbuddy® FORMULATED PUPPY REPLACEMENT at a concentration of 5g per 15ml of warm water.

  • Directions for use

    Mixed Formulated Puppy Replacement can be stored in the fridge for a day or frozen for up to 5 days. Once opened, the rest powder must be refrigerated for up to 3 months, or can be frozen for up to 6 months to preserve freshness.
